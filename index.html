Introduction

My dad and grandad were both Jazz enthousiasts and record collectors with - if I may say so - excellent taste. They were especially interested in Bebop and Hard Bop and owned many early and original pressings. I am now selling these to enable others to enjoy them. All sales will go through Discogs (Fixed pricing, my store) of ebay (Auctions, my handle).
